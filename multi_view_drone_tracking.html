Multi-view Drone Tracking Datasets

Overview

A collection of datasets for tracking drones using multiple camera views, enabling 3D trajectory reconstruction and multi-view tracking.

Key Features

  • Multiple synchronized camera views
  • 3D trajectory ground truth
  • Camera calibration data
  • Indoor and outdoor scenarios
  • Suitable for multi-camera tracking systems

Dataset Components

The collection consists of 5 different datasets:

Dataset 1: Indoor Flight

  • Indoor environment
  • 3 synchronized cameras
  • Controlled lighting conditions
  • Precise camera calibration

Dataset 2: Outdoor Flight

  • Outdoor environment
  • 4 synchronized cameras
  • Natural lighting conditions
  • Longer flight trajectories

Dataset 3: Multi-Drone

  • Multiple drones flying simultaneously
  • 3 synchronized cameras
  • Complex interaction scenarios
  • Individual trajectory ground truth for each drone

Dataset 4: Occlusion Scenarios

  • Specifically designed for occlusion handling
  • 5 synchronized cameras
  • Various obstacles creating occlusions
  • Challenging tracking scenarios

Dataset 5: Winter Conditions

  • Outdoor environment with snow
  • 4 synchronized cameras
  • Challenging weather conditions
  • Low visibility scenarios

Strengths

  • Unique multi-view perspective
  • Precise 3D ground truth
  • Supports advanced tracking algorithms
  • Well-calibrated camera systems

Limitations

  • Smaller scale than single-view datasets
  • Limited environmental diversity
  • Specialized equipment requirements
  • Complex preprocessing needed
